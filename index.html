Frequently Asked Questions

What is a psychologist?

A psychologist is a mental health professional who helps people understand and manage their thoughts, feelings, and behaviors.

How do I know if I need a psychologist?

If you're experiencing persistent emotional difficulties, relationship issues, or feeling stuck in life, a psychologist can help.

How do I choose the right psychologist?

Consider their specialization, experience, and approach. Our matching system helps find the best fit for your needs.

How much does therapy cost?

Individual sessions start at $50 for 50 minutes. Couples sessions are $75 for 90 minutes.
